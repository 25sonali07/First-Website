    • Sharda University

    The Sharda Group of Institutions has been a provider of world class education since 1996. It is the largest educational group based in Uttar Pradesh, having 13,000+ students and 1200+ faculty strength. It has 47,500+ alumni who are today leaders in their realms. With state-of-the-art campuses in Agra, Mathura, and Greater Noida, the group has transformed the perception of education by adopting a broader approach and focusing on overall development of an individual. Spread across 180 acres of land with 3.5 million square feet of infrastructure, the group institutions has earned the trust and respect as a progressive education provider with successful alumni and prolific faculty.

    School of Engineering & Technology (SET)


    SET at Sharda University offers B.Tech, BCA, B.Sc, M.Tech, MCA, and PhD programs in various disciplines including Computer Science, Electrical & Electronics, Electronics & Communication, Aerospace, etc. For admission in B.Tech, JEE and UPSEE scores of the applicants are considered. Moreover, the university conducts an online test named SUAT followed by personal interviews for the candidates who don't appear in JEE or UPSEE.

    School of Business Studies (SBS)


    SBS at Sharda University offers different programmes including BBA, B.Com (Hons), BA Economics, MBA, PhD and integrated programs in several disciplines. MAT/XAT scores are validated for the admission of MBA applicants. University also conducts an online test named SUAT for the candidates who don't appear in MAT/XAT.

    School of Architecture & Planning (SAP)


    SAP at Sharda is constituted to provide the programmes like B.Arch, M.Arch and PhD in Architecture. A NATA qualified candidate with 50% marks in 10+2 (with Mathematics) is eligible for B.Arch.

    School of Media, Film and Entertainment (SMFE)


    SMFE at Sharda University is established to offer the creative course like B.A (Journalism & Mass Communication), B.A (Film and Television Production), M.A (Journalism & Mass Communication), M.A (Advertising, Public Relations and Corporate Communications), PhD in Mass Communication (Full Time/Part Time), and many more.

    School of Law (SOL)


    Sharda University also includes SOL to impart the course like LLB and LLM. The university also offers integrated programs including BA LLB and B.COM LLB. Sr. Secondary (10+2) with 50% marks is the eligibility criteria for LLB.

    School of humanities and Social Sciences (SHSS)


    University runs the programmes like B.Sc. Yoga, BA English, MA English, and PhD English under the flagship of SHSS. The certificate and advance certificate courses in foreign languages are also offered in SHSS.

    School of Dental Sciences (SDS)


    School of Dental Sciences offers BDS and MDS programs in 9 different departments of dentistry. The admission guidelines formed by DCI (Dental Council of India) are followed here.

    School of Nursing Sciences & Research (SNSR)


    SNSR offers the courses like General Nursing & Midwifery (GNM), B.Sc (Nursing), M.Sc (Nursing). The admissions are subject to eligibility criteria (as per the guidelines issued by Indian Nursing Council) and seat availability.

    School of Allied Health Sciences (SAHS)


    SAHS runs different programmes related to allied health science, such as Bsc Medical laboratory Technology (BMLT), Bachelor in Physiotherapy (BPT), Bachelor of Optometry,Dialysis DOTT, DMLT, M.Sc - Clinical Research and other UG & PG programmes in different disciplines.

    School of Medical Sciences & Research (SMSR)


    Sharda University also maintains a separate school for offering the medical science and research programmes encompassing M.B.B.S., MS, MD, and M.Sc in different streams. Admission in MBBS, MS and MD are subject to MCI guidelines.

    School of Basic Sciences & Research (SBSR)


    The undergraduate and postgraduate programs in science are run under SBSR. Courses like B.Sc and M.sc are offered in different streams. Moreover, the university offers PhD programmes for science researchers.

    School of Education (SOE)


    School of Education at Sharda University conducts B.Ed and integrated programmes like BA B.Ed and B.Sc B.Ed. Graduation with minimum 50% marks is the minimum eligibility criteria for B.Ed whereas a student with minimum 50% marks at senior secondary level can take admission in integrated programme.

    School of Pharmacy (SOP)


    SOP at Sharda University is recognised by Pharmacy Council of India (PCI) for curriculum it has designed for different programmes including B. Pharm, D. Pharm, and PhD in Pharmacy. Eligibility criteria for the courses other than PhD are Sr. Secondary (10+2) in PCM/PCB with 50% marks.
