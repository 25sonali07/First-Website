    • Academic Facilities


    Sharda University provides a magnificent and comprehensive range of facilities within the campus for its students. The classrooms at Sharda University are contemporary, equipped with ceiling mounted LCD projectors and computers. The university frequently organises National & International level Conferences and Seminars on various themes and disciplines to promote industry interaction for its students.

    The auditoriums at Sharda are up to date with state-of-the-art multimedia systems which can be used for presentations, seminars, fests and other academic events. The various libraries are well equipped with a vast number of books, journals, magazines and newspapers for students. The development of state-of-the-art infrastructure and technical facilities has been a continuous process at Sharda University. The laboratories provide practical exposure to various subjects and give an opportunity to the students to experiment with what they have learnt in classrooms.


    Computer Labs

    The University recognizes the key role that IT plays in teaching as well as research. Therefore, it is committed to providing computing facilities of the highest standards. A high-speed optical fiber network of 1Gbps through two ISPs with wireless redundancy allows all students to access a range of services across our entire campus including student hostels with more than 200 access points.

    Seminar Rooms

    The University has multiple Seminar Rooms equipped with the latest audiovisual facilities which act as a common ground for students and faculty. The auditoriums and seminar rooms are extensively setup for regular interfaces, guest lectures, presentations, conferences, cultural events and other academic activities. The auditoriums and seminar rooms provide an excellent environment to support teaching and learning for both staff and students with complete audio-visual, sound and lighting support and stadium-style seating.


    Highlights

  • Perfect facility for conducting seminars, conferences and presentations at a big scale

  • Easily accessible and functional rooms
  • Highly spacious with state-of-the-art multimedia systems
  • Fully air-conditioned rooms
  • Equipped with ceiling-mounted LCD projectors and computers

    • Health Care Facilities

    Sharda University offers medical facilities to all its students, teaching and non-teaching staff through Sharda Hospital. Equipped with modern amenities, advanced technological equipment, and a spirit to promote community well-being, the hospital is a preferred destination for healthcare nationally and internationally. Moreover, the hospital is a broad platform for the students to avail rich and vast opportunities to pursue collaborative and interdisciplinary education and independent study projects in the field of medical education.


    Highlights

  • Access to a world class 900 bedded multi-speciality hospital.

  • Availability of all modern healthcare facilities like General Medicine, ICU, Trauma centre, 3 Tesla MRI, CT Scan, ECG, X-Ray and Laboratory Investigation.

  • Opportunities to visit healthcare facilities, learn from experts and interact with other experienced students.

  • Health insurance cover for all students is one of the additional services under active consideration.

  • Counselling on health, diet and lifestyle-related issues.

    • Recording studios (Multimedia development lab)

    Sharda Univerity is a catalyst for collaboration and experimentation in the field of arts, science and humanities. Through its Recording studios and Multimedia development lab, it provides a platform where creative thinkers come together from across disciplines to exchange ideas, explore processes, methods, and develop new art forms. The studios are available for use by students and faculty and serve as a creative playground where they are encouraged to explore their artistic expression by making use of the tools and technology at hand.

    Highlights
  • Acoustically treated rooms

  • Collection of audio plugins, video systems, and various other professional tools

  • Multiple leading analogue sound effects and processors

  • Complete pro tools system

  • Extensive range of microphones
